AI Policy: Less Than Zero

At Less Than Zero, authenticity and human connection are central to our storytelling and creative expression. To maintain these values, we recognize the importance of responsible AI usage. This policy ensures transparency, upholds integrity, and drives excellence in all areas where AI intersects with our work.

Effective Date

This AI Policy becomes effective as of July 9, 2026.

Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish clear guidelines for the use of artificial intelligence tools within Less Than Zero. It aims to promote integrity and accountability while maximizing the benefits that can be derived from integrating intelligent technologies into our workflows.

Scope

This policy applies to all staff members, partners, contractors, and third-party vendors who contribute or collaborate on projects involving AI technology at Less Than Zero.

Definitions

  • Algorithm: A set of rules used in a computer program as procedures to solve problems.
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines such as learning from data and past actions, reasoning based upon this information, planning future activities, recognizing patterns, and making decisions.

Guidelines

1. Transparency

  • All applications or services using AI must disclose any use of synthetic content to users.
  • When distributing material enhanced with AI tools, include a disclaimer that acknowledges its enhancement.
  • Maintain detailed records on how AI systems are deployed across projects.

2. Ethics

  • Ensure the ethical procurement and processing of data necessary for training AI models.
  • Be vigilant about detecting biases within algorithms and take steps to mitigate them as best as possible.
  • Establish measures to prevent misuse or abuse of autonomous features in applications where such risks exist.

3. Data Privacy

  • Strictly adhere to legal requirements governing the collection, storage, use, transfer and sharing of personal information used within AI systems.
  • Regular privacy impact assessments must be conducted when implementing significant changes involving AI technology at Less Than Zero.

4. Quality Assurance for Content Created or Enhanced Using AI

  • Implement rigorous quality control procedures ensuring that any final content remains aligned with our brand identity and editorial standards regardless if it has been enhanced through automated processes.

5. Training & Support

  • Provide comprehensive training materials aimed at educating employees about the principles articulated herein as well as practical implementation strategies for working responsibly with AI technologies.

6. Monitoring Compliance

  • Appoint a representative or committee dedicated to monitoring adherence across all areas covered by this policy and facilitating updates when needed.

Reporting Violations

Any individual who believes they have observed noncompliance with any provision outlined within this document should promptly report their concerns through established channels.
